Dear supporter,

When I started this project, I honestly wasn't sure anyone would care. I'm just one person, with a secondhand laptop, trying to solve a problem I felt deeply — YouTube is an amazing place, but it's designed to steal your time.

Your contribution — however big or small — means I don't have to give up. The $25 Google Play developer fee is a tiny number in most people's lives, but it was a real barrier for me. You removed it.

This app is going to help people study better, focus more, and reclaim hours of their lives. And when someone opens it for the first time and feels that relief — that's because of you.

I promise to build something you'll be proud to have supported. Every update, every bug fix, every feature — I'll carry your trust with me.
